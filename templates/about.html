About Walla Jasho Loaders

Walla Jasho Loaders is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) focused on managing sand and stone loading operations in a transparent and organized way.

🎯 Our Mission

  • To improve fairness in loading operations
  • To digitally manage trucks and workers
  • To ensure proper payment tracking
  • To reduce manual errors in operations

🚛 What We Do

  • Truck registration and tracking
  • Sand & stone loading management
  • Payment and income tracking
  • Daily operational reporting
